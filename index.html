    About Me

    I'm Brock, a post-production specialist with experience as an archivist. My post-production work includes audio cleaning, editing, designing, and mixing.
    I'm detail-oriented and driven by the process - I love exploring every auditory nook and cranny of my clients' ideas. Having performed the entire sound-to-post pipeline, I've developed a detailed understanding for where practical needs meet creative desires.
